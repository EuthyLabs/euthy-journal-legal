About the App

Euthy Journal helps users track their mood, health data, and daily thoughts in a completely private environment. We believe your journal should be for your eyes only, which is why we provide tools to help you own your data.

Cloud Sync & Backups

To ensure you never lose your memories, Euthy Journal integrates with Google Drive. This integration allows you to:

  • Create secure, encrypted backups of your journal entries.
  • Synchronize your data seamlessly across multiple Android devices.
  • Maintain total control by storing data in your own private Google account.

Euthy Journal's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
